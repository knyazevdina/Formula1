F1 Drivers

Formula 1 drivers are some of the most skilled racers in the world, competing at the highest level of motorsport. Each season, around 20 drivers represent different teams and race in events called Grands Prix across the globe. Famous drivers include champions like Max Verstappen and Lewis Hamilton, as well as talented competitors such as Charles Leclerc and Lando Norris. These drivers must combine speed, precision, and strategy while driving cars at extremely high speeds, often exceeding 300 km/h. Their performance, teamwork, and consistency throughout the season determine who becomes the world champion.
