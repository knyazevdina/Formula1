Formula 1

Click on the car and you will be redirected to the official Formula 1 site

Formula 1 (often called F1) is the highest level of international car racing. It’s a global championship where the fastest drivers in the world compete in extremely advanced, single-seat race cars.
Here’s the idea in simple terms:

  • Cars: Specially designed, super-fast racing cars (they can go over 300 km/h).
  • Drivers: Only about 20 drivers compete each season, representing different teams.
  • Teams: Big names like Ferrari, Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team, and Red Bull Racing build and race their own cars.
  • Races (Grands Prix): Held in different countries (like Monaco, Italy, Japan).
  • Points system: Drivers earn points based on where they finish, and at the end of the season there’s:
  • a Driver Champion
  • a Team (Constructor) Champion

F1 is popular because of:

  1. insane speed and technology
  2. strategy (pit stops, tire choices, timing)
  3. rivalries between drivers and teams
